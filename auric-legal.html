Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Auric ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Auric mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the best mindfulness experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account via email or Google Sign-In, we collect your email address and authentication tokens.
  • Profile Data: You may choose to provide a username and upload a profile picture.
  • App Usage & Wellness Data: We track your breathing session durations, total minutes practiced, daily streaks, preferred exercises, and self-reported mood check-ins.
  • Device Permissions: With your explicit permission, we may access your device's Photo Gallery (strictly for uploading profile pictures) and send Local Notifications (for daily mindfulness reminders).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to operate and improve the Auric experience, specifically to:

  • Maintain your account and sync your progress across devices.
  • Display your chosen username, profile picture, and total breathing minutes on the Global Leaderboard.
  • Calculate your daily streaks and personal bests.
  • Deliver scheduled local notifications to remind you to practice.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We utilize trusted third-party services to run the app securely:

  • Supabase: Used for secure database hosting, user authentication, and profile image storage.
  • Google: Used to facilitate Google Sign-In authentication.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account entirely at any time using the "Delete Account" button in the "Danger Zone" of the app's Settings menu. Initiating this process will permanently and irreversibly erase your account credentials, session history, and leaderboard data from our servers.
